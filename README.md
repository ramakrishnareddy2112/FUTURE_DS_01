# Business Sales Performance Analytics

Internship Project – Future Interns (Data Science & Analytics)

## Task 1 Objective

Analyze business sales data to identify:

* Revenue trends over time
* Top selling products
* High value categories
* Regional sales performance

## Dataset Used

Superstore Sales Dataset containing order information, product categories, regions, sales values, quantity, discount, and profit.

## Tools Used

Python
Pandas
Matplotlib
VS Code

## Key Insights

* The Technology category generated the highest overall sales.
* The West region produced the highest total revenue among all regions.
* Certain products contribute significantly more revenue compared to others.
* Sales show variation across different months, indicating possible seasonal demand.

## Business Recommendations

* Focus marketing efforts on high-performing product categories such as Technology.
* Expand sales strategies in strong-performing regions to increase revenue.
* Monitor monthly sales trends to plan promotions and stock management.
* Analyze top-selling products to replicate successful sales strategies.
